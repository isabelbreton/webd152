About Us

Our History

Pacific Trails Resort got it's start in 1995 when our founder, Isabel Breton, visited the Mongolian city of Ulaanbaatar and stayed in her very first yurt just outside of the city. She had never enjoyed camping growing up but still wanted to experience nature and found that staying in a yurt offered the experiences she was missing. Upon returning to the United States, she made it her mission to share her experiences with others and showcase that camping can be new, exciting, and luxurious. That's when she decided to bring luxury camping and yurts to the same California coast that she visited often as a child.

Our Mission

We strive to reinvent your idea of camping. We understand that sometimes a vacation needs to be luxurious, relaxing, and a change of pace from busy city life. Gone are the days of pitching tents, hole in the ground toilets, and prepackaged camping food. Pacific Trails Resort offers state of the art yurts with electricity and running water. We offer hot breakfast every morning and plenty of activities to keep you entertained. We want you to leave Pacific Trails Resort relaxed and refreshed.

What to Expect

Pacific Trails Resort is a coastal escape from busy day to day life. We offer a natural escape to the California Coast without sacrificing luxuries such as running water and hot meals. We offer both solo and guided hiking tour for those looking to be active during their stay with us. We also offer bird watching for those who want to relax and enjoy the scenery.

Visit Us

Come visit us anytime! We are open all year as each season on the California coast has something different to offer. Check out our Reservations page and book a stay with us!
